v1.1   2026-03-23

== v1.1: Dye Manipulation Update ==
-> Blocks:
- Added 6 new blocks (8 total)
- 3 Workstations, see below
- 3 Flowers, see below

-> Workstations:
- Bleaching Machine, crafted, turns dyed items into their white version, 200 ticks/item
- Blacking Machine, crafted, turns dyed items into their black version, 200 ticks/item
- Dye Removal Machine, crafted, turns certain dyed items into their colorless version (such as Terracotta), 100 ticks/item

-> Flowers:
- Added Sea Holly, found near Beaches and Rivers, common, used for Dye
- Added Hellebore, found in Forests, used for Dye
- Added Black Rampion, found in Forests, used for Dye

-> Recipes:
- 39 new recipes (41 total)
- White Tulip now gets crafted into White Dye instead of Light Gray Dye
- Blue Dye, Purple Dye from Sea Holly
- Green Dye, Lime Dye from Hellebore
- Black Dye, Brown Dye from Black Rampion
- Bleaching Machine, Blacking Machine, Dye Removal Machine
- Bleaching: Banners, Beds, Candles, Carpets, Concrete, Concrete Powder, Dyes, Stained Glass, Stained Glass Panes, Terracotta, Wool
- Bleaching: White Tulip from Orange Tulip, Pink Tulip, Red Tulip
- Blacking: Banners, Beds, Candles, Carpets, Concrete, Concrete Powder, Dyes, Stained Glass, Stained Glass Panes, Terracotta, Wool
- Dye Removal: Candles, Glass, Glass Panes, Terracotta

-> Other:
- Changed mod display name from easydyes to Easy Dyes
- Dye Station: added dye silhouettes to GUI
